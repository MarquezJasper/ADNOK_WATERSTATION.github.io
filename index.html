About Us

The ADNOK Water Refilling Station Order and Delivery System provides convenience to the buyers not only to the target location but in each Barangay who wants to order and deliver. Water Refilling Station Order and Delivery System will allow users to place order for water and to allow the water refilling station to handle delivery to customers. This system will help them to order water easily and conveniently without having to go to the water refilling station. This system is to design and develop a business that will serve as a platform wherein transaction for placing orders and deliveries for water refilling station are done.
